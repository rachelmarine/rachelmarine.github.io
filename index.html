Hello, I'm Rachel Marine, technical lead of the Division of Viral Diseases Next-Generation Sequencing Core Laboratory at CDC! In graduate school, I spent several years working in the lab of Eric Wommack, delving deep into the viral dark matter! At the CDC, I now apply my expertise in next-generation sequencing/metagenomics towards surveillance and exploration of human pathogens.

Check out the ORCID, Google Scholar and ResearchGate links for more information on microbial ecology, virology, next generation sequencing and bioinformatics research.

Contact email- rmarine@cdc.gov
