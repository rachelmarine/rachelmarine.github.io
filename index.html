Hello, and thanks for stopping by my personal website! I'm Rachel Marine, a PhD candidate at the University of Delaware. For the past several years, I've been working in the lab of Eric Wommack, delving deep into the viral dark matter! My research has revolved around viral metagenomics, both in construction of metagenomes and analysis, to further our understand of viral communities.

Please feel free to check out my CV, which highlights my background in microbial ecology, next generation sequencing and bioinformatics.
